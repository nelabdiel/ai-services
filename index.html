Why AI Leadership Now?

AI is no longer optional—but neither is risk management, regulatory compliance, or strategic alignment. I help organizations translate AI hype into real-world value, with a strong focus on privacy, security, and responsible innovation.

Services Offered

AI Strategy & Roadmapping

  • Identify high-impact AI opportunities aligned with your organization's mission and workflows
  • Prioritize where to build, buy, or wait on AI investments
  • Create realistic, compliant, and forward-looking AI adoption plans
  • Coordinate across technical and executive teams to ensure buy-in and scalability

AI Governance & Risk Frameworks

  • Develop governance plans that address privacy, bias, and model accountability
  • Align your systems with standards like HIPAA, EU AI Act, Executive Orders and NIST RMF
  • Create audit trails, documentation workflows, and model validation pipelines
  • Support compliance reviews and internal/external oversight processes

LLM & Agent Oversight

  • Evaluate risks, benefits, and infrastructure needs for deploying generative AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, custom agents)
  • Establish usage guidelines, logging protocols, and safety boundaries
  • Support implementation of retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), API-based copilots, or agentic workflows
  • Ensure agent deployment aligns with user transparency, data protection, and organizational risk appetite

Deployment & Infrastructure Review

  • Review pipelines for data ingestion, transformation, vectorization, and model inference
  • Evaluate infrastructure readiness (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes, cloud service security)
  • Assess the robustness, reproducibility, and monitoring practices of deployed models
  • Identify potential failure modes and implement fail-safes or fallback mechanisms

Executive Advisory

  • Advise senior leadership on AI investment, hiring, vendor selection, and organizational alignment
  • Design AI literacy and training programs tailored to board members and non-technical executives
  • Serve as a fractional member of your leadership team (CAIO model) without requiring a full-time commitment
  • Translate technical complexity into strategic clarity

Independent AI Evaluation & Expert Services

In addition to advisory and strategy work, I offer independent expert evaluation of AI systems.

When Is This Useful?

  • Litigation or disputes involving algorithmic decision-making
  • Audits or impact assessments for high-risk AI systems
  • Procurement due diligence and public-sector vendor evaluation
  • Healthcare, finance, or legal settings where privacy, explainability, or harm mitigation is essential

Roles I Can Serve

  • Expert Witness – Provide reports, affidavits, or court testimony in cases involving AI use
  • Independent Evaluator – Conduct neutral assessments of AI systems for bias, privacy, safety, or regulatory alignment
  • Technical Advisor to Counsel – Help legal teams or compliance officers understand model behavior and risks
  • Advisory Board Member – Support startups, nonprofits, or mission-aligned orgs on AI strategy and governance
  • Public Contributor – Participate in grant review panels, working groups, or innovation initiatives
  • Speaker – Deliver guest lectures, keynotes, or policy briefings on AI ethics, governance, and adoption

With experience in federal agencies, healthcare systems, and AI governance, I bring both technical rigor and regulatory fluency to this work.
